About Me

I am currently in my third year of pursuing a Ph.D. at the Center for Digital Innovation, Georgia State University. My research focus revolves around the organizational applications of Artificial Intelligence within the realm of Information Systems. During the initial two years of my doctoral journey, I received extensive training in research methodologies and empirical techniques.

Before embarking on my Ph.D. program, I completed my graduate studies, earning a business degree from the prestigious Indian Institute of Management Calcutta (IIM C). My prior professional experience spans four years in the Technology Services Industry, where I engaged in roles related to sales enablement, process transformation, business analysis, research, and cross-functional stakeholder management.

I eagerly anticipate the remaining phases of my doctoral program, where I will have the privilege of collaborating closely with Arun Rai, Yu-Kai Lin and other esteemed faculty members within my department.
